Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with Vivid Craft

How long does a typical project take?
Most logo designs are delivered within 24-48 hours. Complex branding projects may take 3-5 days. We always prioritize quality while maintaining speed.
What files will I receive?
You'll receive all essential formats: AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, JPG, and SVG. Everything you need for print, web, and future editing is included.
Do you offer unlimited revisions?
Yes! Our Business Pro and Enterprise packages include unlimited revisions. We work until you're 100% satisfied with the final result.
What if I don't like the initial concepts?
We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the initial direction, we'll create new concepts at no extra charge until we nail the vision.
How do I get started?
Simply click the "Let's Talk" button or message us on WhatsApp. We'll discuss your project, provide a quote, and begin immediately upon approval.
